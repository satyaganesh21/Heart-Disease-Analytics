About the Project

Transforming complex clinical metrics into strategic health solutions

Problem Statement & Objectives

Analyzing large-scale health metadata related to heart disease—such as patient demographics, medical histories, lifestyle choices, and clinical metrics—requires advanced diagnostic infrastructure. Our project uses Business Intelligence pipelines to transform raw data points into interactive clinical models, highlighting core risk factors to support decision-making for medical systems, policymakers, and individuals alike.
